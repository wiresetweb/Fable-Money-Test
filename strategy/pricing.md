# Pricing

| **Tool** | Standard tool | **$350 flat, no monthly** | 50% deposit / 50% on delivery (DECISIONS #8) |

Any sale from either line counts toward the $1,000 / 14-day goal. A single Tier 2 website ≈ 2.3 tools.

## Tool price points
| Tier | Price | Terms | What |
|------|-------|-------|------|
| Standard tool | **$350 flat** | 50% deposit / 50% on delivery | Single-purpose tool within the 5 scope boundaries (see offer.md) |
| Complex build | **Custom fixed quote, $600–$1,500** | 50/50, timeline stated up front | Fails one or more scope boundaries. Quote = honest estimate of build days × ~$300/day, rounded to a clean number |
| Follow-up addition | **$100–$250 flat** | Paid up front | Post-delivery feature add to an existing client's tool. Keeps "scope discipline" polite: out-of-scope asks become these |

## Rationale
- $350 is an impulse-purchase price for a business owner (less than one small job's revenue for most trades) while still being meaningful toward the $1,000 goal: 3 standard clients = $1,050 = mission complete.
- The custom tier exists so the complexity valve (offer.md) has somewhere to send people — "no" becomes "yes, at the honest price."
- Follow-up pricing turns scope creep into revenue instead of friction.
